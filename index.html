Overview

The Bengal famine of 1943 was one of the most devastating tragedies of colonial India. It claimed over 3 million lives due to a catastrophic combination of policy failure, war, and social injustice. This website brings together personal stories, causes, timelines, and untold truths to memorialize those lost and educate future generations.
