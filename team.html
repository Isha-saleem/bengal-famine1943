This website was built entirely by me, combining technical development with deep historical research. I selected the Bengal Famine of 1943 because of its harrowing impact, lack of awareness in public discourse, and the powerful stories of human resilience and suffering it revealed. Through this project, I aimed to educate, memorialize, and provoke deeper thought around neglected historical tragedies.
